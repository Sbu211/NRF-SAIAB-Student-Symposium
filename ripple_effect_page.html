Key Speakers

Professor R. Dorrington

Professor Dorrington is based in the Department of Biochemistry and Microbiology at Rhodes University. As DST/RNF SARChI Professor in Marine Natural Products (MNPs) Research, Prof. Dorrington leads a large, multidisciplinary research group active across the broad field of marine biodiscovery, including marine biology, microbial ecology, microbial genomics through to marine natural products chemistry and antiviral bioassay development.

Professor Anusha Rajkaran

Professor Anusha Rajkaran is based in the Department of Biodiversity and Conservation Biology at the University of the Western Cape. Her research explores the functioning of estuarine habitats, including salt marshes and seagrass, the ecosystem services they provide, and the impacts of microplastic and chemical pollutants. With work spanning much of South Africa’s coastline, she leads a dynamic research team of postgraduate students and postdoctoral fellows focused on understanding and protecting these vital ecosystems.

About the Symposium

The Ripple Effect: Impactful Science is a premier symposium hosted by NRF-SAIAB, dedicated to advancing aquatic research and its real-world applications. Our mission is to bring together researchers, scientists, and environmental advocates to share innovative findings, foster interdisciplinary collaboration, and drive solutions for sustainable aquatic ecosystems. We aim to highlight research that not only advances scientific understanding but also creates measurable impacts on conservation, policy, and community engagement for the health of our planet’s oceans, rivers, and lakes.
